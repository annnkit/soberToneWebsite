About SoberTone

We're on a mission to make mental health support accessible to everyone through innovative AI technology, combining the power of personalized voice interaction with evidence-based therapeutic approaches.

Our Story

SoberTone was born from a simple yet powerful realization: millions of people struggling with addiction lack access to immediate, personalized support during their most vulnerable moments. Traditional rehabilitation methods, while valuable, are often expensive, inaccessible, and unable to provide real-time assistance when cravings and triggers strike.

Our founder, Ankit Jangir, combined his expertise in AI/ML with his passion for behavioral psychology to create a solution that bridges this critical gap. By leveraging the power of familiar voices and evidence-based therapeutic techniques, SoberTone provides comfort and guidance exactly when it's needed most.

We believe that recovery should be supported by technology that understands human emotion and responds with empathy. Our AI-driven platform doesn't just provide generic advice – it learns from each individual's journey and adapts to provide increasingly personalized support.

Our Mission, Vision & Values

Mission

To make mental health support accessible, affordable, and effective for everyone struggling with addiction by providing personalized, AI-powered voice assistance that offers comfort and guidance during critical moments.

Vision

A world where no one has to face addiction alone, where technology serves as a compassionate companion in the recovery journey, and where personalized mental health support is available to all who need it.

Values

Compassion: Every interaction is guided by empathy and understanding

Innovation: Continuously advancing our technology for better outcomes

Accessibility: Making support available to everyone, regardless of circumstances

Privacy: Protecting user data with the highest security standards

Concept & Research
