# SoberTone Website Interaction Design

## Interactive Components

### 1. AI Voice Simulator
- **Location**: Main hero section
- **Function**: Interactive demo of SoberTone's voice-based support system
- **Interaction**: Users can click on different emotional states (craving, anxiety, depression) and hear/see simulated supportive responses in different voice options
- **Visual**: Waveform animations and voice selection interface

### 2. Progress Tracker Visualization
- **Location**: Objectives section
- **Function**: Interactive timeline showing recovery journey milestones
- **Interaction**: Clickable milestones that reveal detailed information about each stage of addiction prevention and recovery
- **Visual**: Animated progress bar with milestone markers

### 3. Impact Calculator
- **Location**: Market opportunity section
- **Function**: Interactive tool showing potential lives saved and economic impact
- **Interaction**: Users can input different scenarios (number of users, success rates) to see projected impact
- **Visual**: Real-time updating charts and statistics

### 4. Team Member Cards
- **Location**: About page
- **Function**: Interactive team profiles with expertise areas
- **Interaction**: Hover effects reveal detailed backgrounds, click for full bio modal
- **Visual**: Flip card animations with professional photos and expertise tags

## User Journey Flow

1. **Landing**: Hero section with AI voice simulator immediately engages visitors
2. **Understanding**: Progress tracker helps users understand the recovery process
3. **Impact**: Calculator demonstrates the potential societal impact
4. **Trust**: Team section builds credibility with interactive profiles
5. **Action**: Clear CTAs throughout for funding campaign participation

## Animation & Effects

- **Text Effects**: Typewriter animation for key statistics
- **Scroll Motion**: Parallax effects on hero background
- **Hover Effects**: 3D transforms on interactive cards
- **Loading States**: Smooth transitions between content sections
- **Voice Visualization**: Real-time waveform animations during voice demo
